Org Roam

FEATURES

  Private and Portable

All notes are stored locally without any analytics whatsoever. Your data is yours, period. Notes are written in the plaintext org markup, and are always portable.

  Backlinking

Backlinks are at the core of Org Roam. For any note topic, see all the other notes that refer to it.

  Effortless

As all your notes are automatically centralized, you don't have to break flow to decide where a note should go.

  Graph Visualization

Discover surprising and previously unseen connections in your notes with the built-in Graph Network Visualization.

  Extensible

Org Roam is built on top of Emacs and Org Mode, making their vast ecosystems easily integrable.

  Free and Open Source

Org Roam is licensed under the GNU General Public License version 3 or later. Join our Github and contribute to the codebase here.

COMMUNITY

Org Roam has an inclusive community of users passionate about Personal Knowledge Management.

Have an amazing idea for extending Org Roam? Just hop in the forums and kickstart the discussion, or go through the already available community guides for variety of use-cases.
